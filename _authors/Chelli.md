Before workflows were mapped across glowing dashboards and teams moved across digital channels, May Chelli Adasa was already learning how to bring order to motion. She stepped into the world of project management with a natural sense for coordination, aligning people, processes, and purpose the way a conductor guides a complex mechanical symphony.

For over six years, she has directed projects across outsourcing operations, website design and migration, and software development initiatives. Each endeavor, no matter how intricate, is treated as a living system. She organizes teams like interconnected gears, ensures timelines move with steady rhythm, and keeps every component aligned toward completion. Where others see moving parts, she sees flow, balance, and forward momentum.
