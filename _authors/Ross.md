He learned not only how systems function, but how they fail, how they are exploited, and how they must be reinforced to endure. - Sealing breaches swiftly, restoring pressure and balance before momentum is lost.
- Mapping weak joints and stress points to strengthen the engine before failure occurs.
- Surveying the horizon for external signals, gathering intelligence like a navigator reading the stars.


**Hobbies:**
